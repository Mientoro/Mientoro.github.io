About Me

I am a graduate of the University of Calgary with a Bachelor's in Mechanical Engineering and a Minor in Mechatronics. I am fascinated with the new technologies revolutionizing the world around me, and am eager to learn and apply them in my work. Through my passion for learning, I developed a variety of products for a side business that funded a small workshop in which I can explore my interests.

Programming

I first learned to program in middle school in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. After graduating from High School, these skills were applied with Arduino and ESP microcontrollers in C++ and Python.

CAD Design

Once in University, I got access to SolidWorks and Fusion 360. Hundreds of hours have been spent learning both programs. I have particular expertise in designing 3D printed parts.

Program Knowledge

I have spent many hours learning a variety of softwares spanning from 3D design, graphic design, programming, circuit design and film creation.

3D Printing

I currently own several 3D printers. One is converted into a laser cutter, while another is a high end printer. I have experience with most types of filament.
