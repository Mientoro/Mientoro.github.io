Collapsible Light Sabers


As I developed my business, I decided to recreate some popular designs to sell myself. My goal was to make a robust saber that could withstand a beating from playtime. After a few tries, I was able to make a nicely collapsing tube that could withstand hits from an adult swing. Deployable with just a quick shake, these quickly became a popular product.

I am currently waiting on an order of components to add bright lights to these toys. This will add a nice bit of interactivity to the product.
